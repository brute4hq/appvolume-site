🎯 What is PerApp Mixer?

PerApp Mixer is a simple, fast, and low-latency audio mixer for macOS that finally lets you control the volume of each app individually. No subscriptions, no bloat — just clean, intuitive sound control right from your menu bar.

Whether you’re balancing background music with a video call, turning down a noisy browser tab, or keeping notifications quiet while watching a movie — PerApp Mixer gives you precise, per-app volume control that works the way it should.

✨ MVP Features (in development)

🎚 Per-app volume & muteAdjust and mute any app individually.
⚡ Low latencyDesigned for responsiveness — minimal delay between apps and output.
🎵 App bypassKeep certain apps (like DAWs or games) connected directly for zero latency.
🧠 Smart detectionShows only apps that are currently playing sound.
💾 Persistent settingsRemembers your volume preferences per app.
🖥 Menu-bar UIOne click to open, adjust sliders, and close — fast and unobtrusive.
🔌 Virtual audio deviceAppears in macOS System Settings → Sound → Output.
⚙️ Open & freeBuilt in Rust + Swift, released as a free community project.
